Guidance Counselor's Cheat Sheet

Chunking Strategy

Don't write "Study Math." Write specific tasks like "Review Quadratics" or "Do 5 practice problems." Small wins release dopamine!

Pomodoro Timer

Set a timer for 25 minutes of focus, then take a 5-minute break. Your brain can't focus forever without resetting.

Exam Wellness

Sleep is when your brain saves the files. No screens 30 mins before bed. Drink water!
